Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Project Ananta and the investment opportunity.

What is the minimum investment required?

The minimum investment starts at ₹32,50,000 for a 50 sqm premium suite, with a down payment of ₹8,12,500. We also offer flexible payment plans.

How does the buyback guarantee work?

Our buyback guarantee ensures Global One Consulting will repurchase your unit at a predetermined value after a specified period, providing a secure exit strategy.

What does "lifetime free maintenance" include?

It covers common area maintenance, repairs, and upkeep of your apartment, including electrical, plumbing, structural maintenance, and regular cleaning services when unoccupied.

How is the monthly rental income paid?

Monthly rental income is directly deposited into your bank account on a fixed date each month, guaranteed regardless of occupancy for a stable passive income.
