How does the buyback guarantee work?

Our buyback guarantee ensures that Global One Consulting will repurchase your unit at a predetermined value after a specified period. This provides a secure exit strategy for investors who may wish to liquidate their investment in the future.

What does "lifetime free maintenance" include?

Lifetime free maintenance covers all common area maintenance, repairs, and upkeep of your apartment. This includes electrical, plumbing, structural maintenance, and regular cleaning services when the unit is not occupied.

How is the monthly rental income paid?

Monthly rental income of â‚¹25,000 (for 50 sqm units) is directly deposited into your bank account on a fixed date each month. This is guaranteed regardless of occupancy, providing you with a stable passive income stream.
